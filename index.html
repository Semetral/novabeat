Easy to use music commands!

NovaBeat offers easy music commands to enhance the experience of NovaBeat, the bot gets weekly updates, and bug fixes! NovaBeat has a beautiful interface to use, and a very simple command structure.

The bot allows you to do things that costs premium on other music bots! Also whenever we release a new NovaBeat bot, you will be notified and have a chance on gettings the new bots before the offical release date!


Commands

Here is all the commands currently avaible for NovaBeat users.

Music Commands

>play
Use >play to play songs in your current voice channel, this command will not work without a valid name, or if you not in a voice channel.

>pause
Use >pause to pause the song currently playing in the voice channel you are in, if you are not in the voice channel the bot is in this will not work.

>resume
Use >resume to resume the song that you had paused, if you are not in the voice channel the bot is in this command will not work.

>loop
Loops the song currently playing in the voice channel you are in.

>leave
Use >leave for the bot to leave the voice channel you are currently in.

General Commands

>help
Gives you help commands in the channel you are currently in.

>volume
Set the volume of the currently playing music from [1-100]

>search
Search for a song of your choise.

>queue
Adds a certain song of your choise to the queue.
